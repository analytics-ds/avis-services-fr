## What is IPTV and what legal options exist in France?

**IPTV** (Internet Protocol Television) refers to the delivery of television content via the internet protocol, as opposed to terrestrial (DTT) or satellite broadcasting. In France, the legal IPTV market has structured itself around three distinct models by 2026.

Advertising-funded services (AVOD) offer free access in exchange for ad breaks. Subscription platforms (SVOD or TVOD) charge a monthly fee for ad-free access or exclusive content. Finally, operator IPTV is integrated into internet box packages from internet service providers (SFR, Orange, Bouygues, Free).

### Legal framework in France

The use of pirate IPTV services is illegal in France. The law of 27 March 2023 against the piracy of sports content reinforced sanctions and introduced a dynamic blocking mechanism allowing rights holders to have illegal streams blocked in real time. Users of pirate services face fines of up to 300,000€ and prison sentences in the most serious cases.

All services listed in this comparison hold the necessary broadcasting rights on French territory.

## Molotov and Pluto TV: the best free IPTV options

### Molotov

Molotov is the reference free IPTV platform in France, with over 20 million registered users in 2025. The free version provides access to over 40 live channels, including TF1, France 2, France 3, M6, Canal+ (free-to-air), Arte and a selection of thematic channels.

Paid tiers allow access to additional channels and extended replay duration. Molotov Plus (€2.99/month) adds 40 more channels and extends replay to 30 days. Molotov Extended (€13.99/month) unlocks over 200 channels, including sport and cinema packages.

The app is available on all platforms: smartphone, tablet, computer, Smart TV, Apple TV and Chromecast.

### Pluto TV

Pluto TV is a 100% free IPTV service, funded exclusively by advertising (AVOD model). The platform offers over 100 thematic live channels (films, series, documentaries, news, cooking) with no subscription required. The catalogue does not include the main French broadcast channels, but provides a free and legal entertainment alternative for consumers who do not wish to pay for a subscription.

### France.tv

France.tv (formerly Pluzz) is the official platform of France Televisions. It provides live and on-demand access to France 2, France 3, France 4, France 5 and France Info, as well as exclusive content. The service is entirely free and ad-free for on-demand content.

## myCANAL, DAZN and subscription IPTV services

### myCANAL

myCANAL is Canal+'s streaming service, available from €15.99/month for Canal+ access only. The full package including Canal+, cinema channels and sport channels is offered at €25.99/month. The platform integrates over 70 live channels, a 7-day replay and a large VOD library of films and series, including Canal+ original productions.

myCANAL is particularly suited to users who want access to Canal+ without going through a TV box package.

### DAZN

DAZN is Europe's leading sports streaming platform, available at €19.99/month in France. The platform broadcasts the Premier League (English football), LaLiga (Spanish football), Serie A (Italian football), Bundesliga (German football), the Champions League (shared with beIN), boxing and MMA bouts, and tennis tournaments. DAZN does not offer conventional replay but allows viewers to rewatch matches up to 72 hours after broadcast.

### beIN Sports Connect

beIN Sports Connect is available at €15/month and broadcasts Ligue 1 matches (shared with Amazon Prime Video), LaLiga, several European championships, and rugby competitions. The platform offers a replay available for 3 days after events.

### Amazon Prime Video and Disney+

Amazon Prime Video (€6.99/month, included with Prime at €7.99/month) offers live sports broadcasting via add-on channels (Ligue 1 with Ligue 1+, Eurosport, beIN Sports), as well as a library of Amazon Original series and films. Disney+ (€8.99/month Standard, €13.99/month Premium) gives access to the full Disney, Marvel, Star Wars, Pixar and National Geographic catalogue.

## Operator IPTV: the all-in-one solution

French internet service providers (SFR, Orange, Bouygues Telecom, Free) integrate IPTV into their box packages. This solution offers the advantage of access to live television, replay and premium channels in a single package including internet, phone, and TV.

- **SFR**: Play TV packages, 150+ channels, Canal+, Ciné+ and beIN Sports available as options
- **Orange**: TV packages, 200+ channels, intuitive interface, 4K access on select offers
- **Bouygues Telecom**: Bbox packages, 200+ channels, Bbox must with integrated 4K
- **Free**: Freebox, 300+ channels including a premium selection, integrated Molotov access

Operator IPTV is often the most cost-effective solution relative to value delivered, as channels are included in the overall box package. However, it requires being a customer of the relevant provider.
