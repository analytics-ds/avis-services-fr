## Quick summary

1. Back Market is the best-known refurbished marketplace in France.
2. It offers major-brand appliances at 20 to 50 per cent off.
3. Minimum 12 month warranty, 30 day return policy.

## Our Back Market review

Back Market has become the European reference for refurbished goods. The platform gathers more than 1,500 professional sellers, all audited. For appliances, the catalog covers washers, dryers, dishwashers, refrigerators and small appliances.

### Strengths

- Wide product range (large and small appliances)
- Clear grading system (fair, good, excellent)
- Minimum 12 month warranty
- Responsive customer support

### Weaknesses

- Delivery times vary by seller
- Refurbishment quality depends on the seller chosen
- Limited visibility on exact condition before arrival

## Back Market grades compared

| Grade | Appearance | Typical discount |
|---|---|---|
| Fair | Visible marks | 30 to 50 per cent |
| Good | Light marks | 20 to 30 per cent |
| Excellent | Like new | 15 to 25 per cent |

## Our verdict

Back Market remains a safe bet for buying refurbished appliances. The platform's reliability and warranty make it a smart choice for anyone looking to save without sacrificing dependability.
