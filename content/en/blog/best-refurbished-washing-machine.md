## Why choose a refurbished washing machine in 2026

The financial argument is the most immediate. A quality refurbished washing machine (grade A, 12-month warranty) costs between 200 and 500 euros depending on capacity and brand, versus 400 to 900 euros for the same model new. On a 600-euro appliance, the saving often exceeds 300 euros.

The ecological argument is equally strong. Refurbishing a washing machine means avoiding the production of a new appliance and diverting electronic waste from the recycling stream. The carbon footprint of a refurbished appliance is estimated at 50 to 80 % lower than that of an equivalent new appliance.

In 2026, choosing refurbished is no longer a compromise. It is a fully endorsed alternative by professional platforms, which have standardised their quality control processes and aligned their warranties with those of new products.

## Criteria for choosing the best refurbished washing machine

### Capacity and loading type

Capacity is chosen according to household size. For 1 to 2 people, a 6 to 7 kg machine is more than sufficient. Beyond 3 people, targeting 8 to 10 kg avoids multiple cycles and reduces water consumption. For large families, 11 to 12 kg models exist on the refurbished market from brands like Samsung or Whirlpool.

The choice between front-load (drum) and top-load depends on room layout. Top-load machines are more practical in tight spaces and allow adding laundry mid-cycle. Front-load machines allow stacking a tumble dryer on top and generally offer better spin performance.

### Energy rating

On the refurbished market, available energy ratings depend on the age of the appliances in stock. Ratings A, B and C (new EU label post-2021) correspond to the most recent and efficient appliances. Ratings D and below imply higher electricity and water consumption, which can reduce the financial benefit of buying refurbished over time.

A class A washing machine consumes approximately 40 kWh per year (50 standard cycles). At 0.22 €/kWh, that represents less than 9 euros per year. A class C or D appliance may consume twice as much.

### Reconditioning grade and warranty

Grades vary by seller. "Grade A" means the appliance looks like new with no visible signs of use. "Grade B" allows for minor cosmetic marks. "Grade C" shows visible marks but functions perfectly. For a washing machine, grade A or B is recommended: the drum seal integrity and the condition of the drum and gaskets are critical points that warrant an appliance in good visual condition.

The minimum warranty to require is **12 months** with coverage for labour and parts at home. A washing machine, unlike a smartphone, cannot be posted back: at-home after-sales service is a non-negotiable criterion.

## Where to buy a reliable refurbished washing machine

The choice of platform is at least as important as the choice of model. In 2026, four players structure the French market:

- **Murfy**: large appliance specialist, in-house workshop reconditioning, 12-month warranty with at-home after-sales service included. Slightly higher prices than competitors, but the market reference for service quality.
- **Back Market**: multi-seller marketplace, strict quality control, 12-month warranty. Wide choice of brands and models, effective comparison interface.
- **Boulanger / Darty Reconditionné**: combined physical and e-commerce network. Key advantage: in-store pickup, after-sales service accessible in all major cities.
- **Underdog**: rising specialist in refurbished appliances, aggressive prices up to -50 % on new, rotating stock.

## What price to expect for a refurbished washing machine in 2026

Budget depends on capacity, brand and grade. As a guide, price ranges observed in 2026:

- **Entry-level (6-7 kg, grade B/C)**: 150 to 280 €. Brands: Candy, Indesit, Hotpoint, Haier.
- **Mid-range (8-9 kg, grade A/B)**: 280 to 420 €. Brands: Samsung, Bosch, LG, Electrolux.
- **Premium (10 kg and over, grade A)**: 420 to 700 €. Brands: Siemens, Miele, Samsung large formats.

Delivery and installation costs must be added to these prices. Murfy includes delivery and installation in its price. Other platforms charge these services separately (20 to 80 €). Comparing total cost, not just the listed price, is essential for an honest comparison.

## Lifespan and maintenance of a refurbished washing machine

A properly maintained refurbished washing machine can last a further 10 to 15 years. Key habits for extending lifespan:

- Clean the drain filter every 3 months.
- Run a 90 °C programme without laundry once a month to degrease the drum.
- Never overload the machine beyond its rated capacity.
- Regularly check door seal condition to prevent mould.
- Use a descaling product twice a year in hard-water areas.

Appliances reconditioned by certified workshops (Murfy, Satisfix) have undergone full pressure and cycle testing before resale. These controls significantly reduce the risk of breakdown during the first 12 months, which is the most critical period for any household appliance.
