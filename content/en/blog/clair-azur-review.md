## Quick summary

1. Clair Azur is a French high-end spa manufacturer.
2. Its models cover family spas, swim spas and built-in spas.
3. Warranty up to 5 years on the shell.

## Our Clair Azur review

Clair Azur has become a reference in the French spa market. Founded in the 1980s, the brand maintains European manufacturing and relies on premium materials: Aristech shells, reinforced insulation and advanced filtration systems.

### Strengths

- European manufacturing with traceability
- Broad range suited to all uses
- Extended warranty up to 5 years on the shell
- Dense distribution network in France

### Weaknesses

- High prices compared to Asian brands
- Delivery times can be long for custom models
- Add-ons quickly drive up the final bill

## Clair Azur range

| Model | Capacity | Indicative price |
|---|---|---|
| Entry-level | 4 seats | 7,000 to 10,000 euros |
| Mid-range | 5 to 6 seats | 10,000 to 15,000 euros |
| Swim spa | 6 to 8 seats | 15,000 to 25,000 euros |
| High-end | Custom | 20,000 euros and up |

## Our verdict

Clair Azur targets demanding buyers looking for a durable, well-built and highly customizable spa. The budget is significant, but quality and after-sales service justify the investment over the long term.
